What We Do & Where We're Going

Mission Statement

Our mission is: to accomplish the advancement of Freemasonry and ensure quality in the Masonic experience by initiating well-qualified men, providing quality Masonic education and engaging all members in continuing Masonic fellowship and activities.

Vision Statement

Our vision is: to be known as a lodge that ensures the advancement of Freemasonry and the quality of the Masonic experience.

Declaration of Principles

Fort Worth Lodge is a fellowship of brothers united in a common pursuit of virtue. The lodge strives to engage in "best-practices" Freemasonry and works to cultivate an understanding of traditional societies and institutions among the brethren. The lodge is dedicated to the preservation of traditional forms and meaningful human fellowship as a means of transmitting wisdom and improving society.
