About Fort Worth Lodge

Things to Know Before Visiting

  • Our stated meetings are on the 2nd Monday of each month at 7 PM, unless there is a holiday. We meet on floor 3.
  • We meet for dinner before stated meetings at 6 PM on floor 3M.
  • We generally do not eat before our called meetings, which start at 6 PM
  • Our members feel comfortable attending lodge in a wide range of dress, from casual to business formal.
  • We request that attendees wear long pants (no shorts) and closed-toed shoes.
  • Our street address is 1100 Henderson Street, but the entrace to the parking lot is behind the Temple, on Lancaster.
